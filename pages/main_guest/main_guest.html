An App for Finding Friends and Activity Partners

Nowadays, many people want to participate in activities such as exercising, traveling, playing sports, studying, or joining hobby groups. However, they often do not have friends who share the same interests or have matching schedules. As a result, they may miss opportunities or feel uncomfortable doing activities alone. This application is designed to solve that problem by helping users connect with others who share similar interests and are looking to join activities together.

Our Features


Activity Search System

Users can search for activities based on:

  • Type of activity
  • Location
  • Date and time
  • Number of participants

Create an Activity

Users can:

  • Create posts to invite others
  • Set details such as time, location, and participant limit
  • Specify skill level (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
  • Asking Question

Chat System

Participants can communicate to discuss plans and details before meeting.


Review and Rating System

Users can rate each other after completing activities to build trust and ensure safety.
